  • Challah
    • 500 grams flour
    • 4 spoons sugar
    • 1 spoon yeast
    • 1 egg
    • 60 ml oil
    • 200 ml tap water (room temp)
    • teaspoon salt

    Mix and let rest for an hour.

    Make into strips, then turn into challah.

    Let rest for another hour.

    Put coating:

    • teaspoon oil
    • one egg

    Bake at 180°C for 30 minutes.

    Makes2x Challah
  • Meatballs
    • 500 grams ground beef
    • 1 egg
    • 1 spoon mushroom soup powder
    • 1 small potato, mashed
    • 1 zucchini, grated
    • 1 spoon bread crumbs
    • 1 spoon parsley, chopped
    • 1 fried onion, chopped

    Mix all ingredients and fry until cooked through.

  • Pita
    • 300 ml water
    • 500 grams flour
    • 5-7 grams yeast
    • teaspoon salt
    • 1 spoon sugar
    • half teaspoon baking powder

    Mix, knead, and let rest for 2 hours covered.

    Preheat oven with tray inside.

    Shape dough into 150-gram balls and let rest for 10 minutes.

    Flatten and bake directly on the hot tray for about 4 minutes.

    Makes 5
  • Falafel
    • 200 grams dry chickpeas
    • Half pack coriander and/or parsley
    • 1 small onion
    • 1 teaspoon cumin
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 3 cloves garlic
    • Salt & pepper as needed
    • Half hot pepper as needed

    Soak chickpeas in water overnight (12 hours).

    Blend finely, adding a bit of water as needed.

    Fry in boiling oil until golden, or bake at 220°C for 40 minutes.

  • Hummus
    • 200 grams chickpeas (or 450 if already soaked/canned)
    • 1 teaspoon flat soda
    • 80 grams ice
    • 15 grams olive oil
    • 20 grams lemon juice
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 1 teaspoon cumin
    • 175 grams tahini

    Cook soaked chickpeas in water for 3 hours.

    Add onion and 3-4 cloves garlic, and boil for another 1.5 hours.

    Strain and blend quickly with ice.

    Add remaining ingredients and blend until smooth.

    Adjust thickness with tahini and water/oil as desired.
